History

Founded in 2005 by passionate outdoorsmen, Splash Rapids Adventures started small but quickly became known for its thrilling rafting excursions. Through dedication to safety and the environment, we've grown into a premier rafting company, offering unforgettable adventures on rivers across the region. Join us and experience the rush of Splash Rapids Adventures!
